ELIXIR

Logotype for a new programming language.

Elixir is a dynamic, functional programming language designed for building scalable and maintainable applications. Elixir leverages the Erlang VM, known for running low-latency, distributed and fault-tolerant systems, while also being successfully used in web development and the embedded software domain.

CONCEPT

Based on the playful side of the elixir as magic potions, the colors and nuance in a drop shape brings the modernity of language as well as robust and solid feeling of the Erlang.
