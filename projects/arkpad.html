ARKPAD

A new experience designing a house with arkpad.

CONTEXT

Arkpad is the first online platform that gathered products and services in the world of Architecture, Interior Design and Decoration with accurate curation in Brazil. An online reference for Designers and Architects that aimed to create a community of professionals and make the experience of design pleasurable and easier through a community platform and marketplace. More about arkpad >

PROCESS

Arkpad is a known brand in Architecture and Interior Design field as a curated reference. The goal of the project was to make Arkpad a great marketplace and attract new professionals and showrooms. Working on strategy, concept and development we defined the new Arkpad as a better experience to design a house.

Project designed and developed at Jurema software and innovation consultancy | 2014
