SOCCEREX

Mobile app for Soccerex Global Convention.

Soccerex has been bringing together the global football industry to network in a unique commercial environment for over twenty years. From hosting industry leading networking events across the globe to their market insight campaigns, they provide a platform to connect business with the game's key stakeholders. The Soccerex Global Convention is the most renowned event dedicated to the world of football. More about Soccerex >

PROJECT

The fifteenth edition of Soccerex Global Convention took place in Rio de Janeiro, host city of the 2014 FIFA World Cup Final and 2016 Olympics. The Soccerex Global Convention brought together the leading buyers and suppliers in world football for three days of unrivalled insight, networking and business. Soccerex app was designed to improve the experience during the conference, following the events, exhibition and programme. A friendly and simply solution to make participants aware and don't miss the action.
